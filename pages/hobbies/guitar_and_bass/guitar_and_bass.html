GUITAR & BASS

I currently play both the guitar and bass for the sheer joy of it.

I grew up in a musical household where my dad is a singer from Kosova -- so a lot of my influence on music I like to play comes from him. Albanian folk music, and rock music are primarily my choices of songs to learn.

I have a list of other instruments I want to learn as well. Mandolin is one of them since it has a great sound for music such as older Napoleon songs. Another instrument would be the darabuka which is heavily used in Albanian folk music; though I do struggle with more-complex time-signatures like 7/8, 9/8, etc.
